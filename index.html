[ ABOUT ]

StratDots is a real-time strategy war game where you command armies across dynamic battlefields. Choose your side, deploy your forces, and dominate the enemy.

Built with LÖVE2D (Lua), StratDots delivers intense strategic combat with 2 built-in maps, a custom map maker, and challenging AI opponents.

UNIT TYPES

  • INFANTRY - Fast, cheap, and versatile foot soldiers (50 coins)
  • CAVALRY - Rapid mounted units for flanking maneuvers (100 coins)
  • TANKS - Heavy armored units with devastating firepower (200 coins)

STRATEGY TIPS

  • Deploy units along the center line for automatic positioning
  • Capture points generate income - control the map to fund your army
  • Use formations to control unit facing and positioning
  • Artillery emplacements provide long-range fire support
  • Aircraft bombing runs can devastate enemy concentrations
  • Walls slow enemy advances and protect key positions
